The Learning Technology Program Coordinator is responsible for:

Talent LMS

  • Creating accounts, creating classes, managing access, adding students to classes
    • Creating accounts:
      • In the administrator screen, click "add user". Fill in the information. Done.
      • You may need to remind NIH students to check their Duke email. All official Duke business, including logins, should be done through the Duke email
      • Instead of clicking add user, you can just click user to find accounts that have been deactivated to reactivate them. If you are unable to add someone because the email has already been used, they need to be reactivated
    • Creating classes:
      • On the administrator screen, click on "courses". Then find the latest edition of the course you are creating and click the magnet-looking U.
      • Create the course, and rename it to include the current year
      • Add the instructors, Jim, & Steve to the course
      • Eventually, I deactivate previous courses. Some students like to have access for a year or two. Instructors are generally okay with that
    • Managing Access and Adding Students
      • On the instructor screen, choose a course. On the right, choose "Users & Progress"
      • Use the search function to find names and add them to the course
