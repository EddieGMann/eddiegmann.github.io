It has occured where a student from UNC will take a class at Duke. To access the systems, a sponsored account will be needed.

The Learning Technology Program Coordinator is responsible for:

Sponsored Accounts

  • Sometimes, outside people are asked to do some research with the department or take a short course.
  • Creating a sponsored account is very easy
  • Go to the OIT site
    • Request a new guest account
    • Select yourself as the sponsor
    • Choose "Research/Academic Collaborator (requiring access to clinical systems)"
    • For Guest Services, select everything except DukeCard
    • Put in their non-Duke email
    • State the purpose of the account. Generally, it will be research
    • Answer the next two questions. I have always answered "no" to both
    • Agree to the terms and attest that you are not helping a disbarred party
    • Done. Well, your part is done. The sponsored will still need to complete the instructions in the email they receive

You will get many emails about ending sponsorship when the account is about to close.
