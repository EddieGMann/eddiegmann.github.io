For Duke Elements, the Learning Technology Program Coordinator is responsible for:

  • Helping to maintain access to Duke Elements
  • Duke Elements
    • In the top-left corner, click the triple lines
      • Under System Admin -> User Management -> Impersonate
    • Choose the person's profile to edit
    • Under their picture choose "Edit My Profile"
    • Account Settings Page
      • On the account settings page you can update settings, including delegates. (If they need to be added or removed)

For Duke Scholars, the Learning Technology Program Coordinator is responsible for:

  • Duke Scholars
    • You will need to add affiliations for new hires that are not faculty for Biostatistics & Bioinformatics (Department 50000830)
    • You will be emailed with a hire sheet that will tell you which affiliation to add
      • Division of Biostatistics
      • Division of Integrative Genomics
      • Division of Translational Biomedical
      • MMCi (You will probably never add this one)
    • From time to time will need to update or reactivate accounts.
      • Find the account of the person, click on manage profile, from there you can edit or reactivate
